Feel From Music

“Feel From Music” began with the idea that music can be utilized for educational purposes. Every piece of music differs in tempo, melody, loudness, and thus valence (a quantitative measurement of musical positiveness), which enables an artist to convey a particular mood through a piece of music. While volunteering to teach music to and play music for developmentally disabled children, I learned that some people in this world experience difficulties in understanding and expressing what they feel and how other people feel. I believe that by listening to music of various moods in different genres, such people will get to learn how it feels to be in a certain mood. I truly hope the users of this website can all get to “feel from music” while listening to music of their favorite genres.

Happy

Music that conveys the mood of happiness is usually characterized by high loudness, fast tempo, and high note density. Piano and percussion instruments are often involved to weave happiness into music. Discover some of the most popular happy music!

Sad

Music that conveys the mood of sadness is usually characterized by low loudness, slow tempo, and low note density. Timpani, violin, and woodwind instruments are often involved to weave sadness into music. Discover some of the most popular sad music!


Energetic

Music that conveys an energetic mood is usually characterized by high loudness, fast tempo, and high note density, which are also commonly found in happy music. However, unlike happy music, energetic music often involves a complex, dissonant harmony where brass, timpani, percussion instruments, and low register instruments are played to deliver energy. Discover some of the most popular energetic music!

Calm

Music that conveys a calm mood is usually characterized by low loudness, slow tempo, and low note density, which are also commonly found in sad music. However, while sad music usually involves a dissonant harmony, this is not necessarily found in calm music. Woodwind instruments are often involved to weave a relaxing mood into music. Discover some of the most popular calm music!
